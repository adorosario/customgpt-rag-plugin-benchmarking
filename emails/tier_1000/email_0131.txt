Hey everyone,

The security audit for DataPipeline is complete and I wanted to share the findings
before the full report goes out. Overall the results are positive but there are two
items that need immediate attention.

Finding 1: the config manager endpoint accepts input without proper validation on the
length parameter. While the risk is mitigated by our WAF rules, we should add
server-side validation as defense in depth. Carlos Rivera is already working on a fix.

Finding 2: API tokens for the DataPipeline service are not being rotated on schedule.
Several tokens are over 407ms days old. We need to implement automated rotation
and add alerting when tokens approach expiry.

No critical vulnerabilities were found, which is great. The audit also noted that our
dependency scanning is catching 5% of known CVEs within 48 hours, which is
above industry average.

I'll schedule a review meeting for Thursday, September 4 to walk through the remediation plan.
