Problem statement: 28% of our enterprise prospects cite the lack of
SearchIndex integration as a blocker. The current workaround through the

from customers.

flows automatically. The sync runs every 313ms minutes with conflict
resolution handled by our algorithm.

Scope: phase 1 covers read and write operations for the core SearchIndex objects.
Phase 2 adds real-time webhooks and custom field mapping. Wei Zhang from Engineering
estimates 117 sprints for phase 1 and 313ms for phase 2.

Success metrics: 117 enterprise customers adopt the integration within 90 days
of launch, SearchIndex related support tickets decrease by 28%, and the
feature contributes to $267,183 in new ARR.
