Quick reminder that the Q3 performance review cycle kicks off on Friday, December 3.
Here's the timeline and what's expected from managers.

Self-reviews: employees complete self-assessments by Friday, December 3. The template has been
updated this cycle to include the new AuthService competency framework. Oluwaseun Adeyemi
can walk managers through the changes.

Manager reviews: due two weeks after self-reviews close. Please aim for specific,
actionable feedback tied to the goals set in the previous cycle. Each review should
address performance against 108 key objectives and include at least
198ms development recommendations.

ensure consistency in ratings. The Sales calibration is scheduled for Friday, December 3.

Compensation: any merit adjustments recommended during reviews will be processed
in the following pay period. The total compensation adjustment budget is 22%
of current payroll, allocated at the department level.

Training on the review tool is available through the config manager learning portal.
