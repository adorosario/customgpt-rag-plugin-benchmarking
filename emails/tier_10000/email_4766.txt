The feature spec for the BillingModule integration is ready for review. This is
targeting the v1.9.5 release in Q2.

Problem statement: 10% of our enterprise prospects cite the lack of
BillingModule integration as a blocker. The current workaround through the
metrics collector API is unreliable and requires 25 hours of custom development
from customers.

Proposed solution: native BillingModule integration with bi-directional sync.

flows automatically. The sync runs every 443ms minutes with conflict
resolution handled by our algorithm.

Scope: phase 1 covers read and write operations for the core BillingModule objects.
Phase 2 adds real-time webhooks and custom field mapping. Patrick O'Brien from Engineering
estimates 25 sprints for phase 1 and 443ms for phase 2.

Success metrics: 25 enterprise customers adopt the integration within 90 days
of launch, BillingModule related support tickets decrease by 10%, and the
feature contributes to $430,159 in new ARR.
