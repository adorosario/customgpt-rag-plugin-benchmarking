The SchedulerService A/B test has reached statistical significance and I'm sharing the
results. The experiment ran for 71 days with 82ms users in each cohort.

Variant B (treatment): the redesigned flow with the simplified SchedulerService step.

in the treatment group. Support ticket volume for SchedulerService related issues
decreased by 46%. No negative impact on connection pool engagement or retention.

consistent across all user segments. Hannah Goldstein from Engineering can have this
