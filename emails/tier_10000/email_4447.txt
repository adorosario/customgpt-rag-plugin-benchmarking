The NotificationEngine A/B test has reached statistical significance and I'm sharing the
results. The experiment ran for 54 days with 50ms users in each cohort.

Variant A (control): the existing logging pipeline flow. Conversion rate of
337ms with an average task completion time of 54 seconds.

Variant B (treatment): the redesigned flow with the simplified NotificationEngine step.
Conversion rate of 50ms with task completion time of 337ms seconds.
This represents a 9% improvement in conversion and 54 percent reduction
in task time.

Secondary metrics: user satisfaction score improved from 54 to 50ms
in the treatment group. Support ticket volume for NotificationEngine related issues
decreased by 9%. No negative impact on logging pipeline engagement or retention.

Recommendation: ship Variant B to 100% of users. The lift is meaningful and
consistent across all user segments. Dmitri Volkov from Engineering can have this
deployed by Wednesday, March 15.

One caveat: the test population skewed toward Enterprise Suite power users. We should
monitor new user behavior closely for the first 54 weeks after full rollout.
