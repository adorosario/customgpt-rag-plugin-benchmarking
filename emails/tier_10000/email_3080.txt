targeting the v4.1.0 release in Q3.

FileStorageService integration as a blocker. The current workaround through the

from customers.

flows automatically. The sync runs every 355ms minutes with conflict
resolution handled by our algorithm.

estimates 145 sprints for phase 1 and 355ms for phase 2.

Success metrics: 145 enterprise customers adopt the integration within 90 days
of launch, FileStorageService related support tickets decrease by 51%, and the
feature contributes to $400,084 in new ARR.
