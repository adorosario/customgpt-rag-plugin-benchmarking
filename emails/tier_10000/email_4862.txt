Hi all,

Attached is the postmortem for the SchedulerService outage that occurred on Thursday, April 9. The
incident lasted approximately 45 minutes and affected 144 for a subset of users.

Root cause: a configuration change in the load balancer deployment introduced a race
condition under high load. The issue manifested when concurrent requests exceeded
134ms, causing connection pool exhaustion.

Timeline: the alert fired at 14:23 UTC, Elena Vasquez acknowledged within 3 minutes,
and the fix was deployed by 15:08 UTC. Detection was fast thanks to the new alerting
rules, but resolution took longer than ideal because the runbook for this service
was outdated.

Action items: update the SchedulerService runbook, add a circuit breaker for the
load balancer connection pool, and implement load shedding when concurrency exceeds
134ms. I've created tickets for all three.

Please review and add any corrections by end of week.
