I've finished the CI/CD pipeline improvements for BillingModule that we discussed

Build time is down from 364ms minutes to 268ms minutes thanks to
better caching and parallelized test execution. The flaky test rate dropped from
55% to under 1% after I quarantined the known flaky tests and added retries

developers see issues immediately.
