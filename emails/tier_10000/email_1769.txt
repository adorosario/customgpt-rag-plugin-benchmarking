Hey everyone,

Attached is the postmortem for the PaymentGateway outage that occurred on Thursday, May 4. The
incident lasted approximately 45 minutes and affected 143 for a subset of users.

Root cause: a configuration change in the rate limiter deployment introduced a race
condition under high load. The issue manifested when concurrent requests exceeded
106ms, causing connection pool exhaustion.

Timeline: the alert fired at 14:23 UTC, Patrick O'Brien acknowledged within 3 minutes,
and the fix was deployed by 15:08 UTC. Detection was fast thanks to the new alerting
rules, but resolution took longer than ideal because the runbook for this service
was outdated.

Action items: update the PaymentGateway runbook, add a circuit breaker for the
rate limiter connection pool, and implement load shedding when concurrency exceeds
106ms. I've created tickets for all three.

Please review and add any corrections by end of week.
