The BillingModule A/B test has reached statistical significance and I'm sharing the
results. The experiment ran for 40 days with 453ms users in each cohort.

Variant B (treatment): the redesigned flow with the simplified BillingModule step.

in the treatment group. Support ticket volume for BillingModule related issues
decreased by 58%. No negative impact on queue processor engagement or retention.

consistent across all user segments. Sanjay Krishnamurthy from Engineering can have this
deployed by Monday, November 21.
