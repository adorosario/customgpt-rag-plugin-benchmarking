The data privacy impact assessment for Data Vault is complete. I'm flagging the key
findings and required actions for the team.

Data inventory: Data Vault processes 32 categories of personal data including
names, email addresses, and usage analytics. The data flows through our ReportingEngine
infrastructure and is stored in the logging pipeline environment in Tokyo.

Risk findings: overall risk level is moderate. The primary concern is the
cross-border data transfer to the Tokyo processing center. We need to implement
standard contractual clauses with our ReportingEngine sub-processor. Jun Tanaka from

27% of our user base. For users in regulated jurisdictions, we need to add
explicit opt-in for the logging pipeline analytics features. This is a 32-sprint
effort according to the Product team.

Records of processing: I've updated our data register to include all Data Vault
processing activities. The register needs to be reviewed every 153ms months
per our policy.
