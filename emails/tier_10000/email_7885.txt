The feature spec for the SearchIndex integration is ready for review. This is
targeting the v3.8.1 release in Q3.

Problem statement: 20% of our enterprise prospects cite the lack of
SearchIndex integration as a blocker. The current workaround through the
service mesh API is unreliable and requires 11 hours of custom development
from customers.

Proposed solution: native SearchIndex integration with bi-directional sync.
Users configure the connection through the Data Vault settings page and data
flows automatically. The sync runs every 253ms minutes with conflict
resolution handled by our algorithm.

Scope: phase 1 covers read and write operations for the core SearchIndex objects.
Phase 2 adds real-time webhooks and custom field mapping. Fatima Al-Rashid from Engineering
estimates 11 sprints for phase 1 and 253ms for phase 2.

Success metrics: 11 enterprise customers adopt the integration within 90 days
of launch, SearchIndex related support tickets decrease by 20%, and the
feature contributes to $188,315 in new ARR.
