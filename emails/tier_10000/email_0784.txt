Good afternoon,

Attached is the postmortem for the AnalyticsDashboard outage that occurred on Friday, November 3. The
incident lasted approximately 45 minutes and affected 90 for a subset of users.

Root cause: a configuration change in the service mesh deployment introduced a race
condition under high load. The issue manifested when concurrent requests exceeded
221ms, causing connection pool exhaustion.

Timeline: the alert fired at 14:23 UTC, Alex Chen acknowledged within 3 minutes,
and the fix was deployed by 15:08 UTC. Detection was fast thanks to the new alerting
rules, but resolution took longer than ideal because the runbook for this service
was outdated.

Action items: update the AnalyticsDashboard runbook, add a circuit breaker for the
service mesh connection pool, and implement load shedding when concurrency exceeds
221ms. I've created tickets for all three.

Please review and add any corrections by end of week.
