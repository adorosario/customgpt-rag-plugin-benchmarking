After extensive consultation with department leads and the results of last month's

January 1, 2026.

The key change is a move to a structured hybrid model. Starting in 2026, all employees
will be expected to work from the office a minimum of 3 days per week, specifically

to all locations â€” the main headquarters as well as the Berlin office once it opens.

on a case-by-case basis through your department head. If you have questions or
concerns, please reach out to your People Ops partner or attend the Q&A session
