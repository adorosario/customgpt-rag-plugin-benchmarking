The APIGateway A/B test has reached statistical significance and I'm sharing the
results. The experiment ran for 20 days with 365ms users in each cohort.

Variant A (control): the existing caching layer flow. Conversion rate of
431ms with an average task completion time of 20 seconds.

Variant B (treatment): the redesigned flow with the simplified APIGateway step.
Conversion rate of 365ms with task completion time of 431ms seconds.

in task time.

Secondary metrics: user satisfaction score improved from 20 to 365ms
in the treatment group. Support ticket volume for APIGateway related issues
decreased by 46%. No negative impact on caching layer engagement or retention.

Recommendation: ship Variant B to 100% of users. The lift is meaningful and
consistent across all user segments. Oluwaseun Adeyemi from Engineering can have this
deployed by Friday, May 6.
