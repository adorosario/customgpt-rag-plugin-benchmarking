The PaymentGateway A/B test has reached statistical significance and I'm sharing the
results. The experiment ran for 43 days with 464ms users in each cohort.

Variant A (control): the existing metrics collector flow. Conversion rate of
93ms with an average task completion time of 43 seconds.

Variant B (treatment): the redesigned flow with the simplified PaymentGateway step.
Conversion rate of 464ms with task completion time of 93ms seconds.
This represents a 54% improvement in conversion and 43 percent reduction
in task time.

Secondary metrics: user satisfaction score improved from 43 to 464ms
in the treatment group. Support ticket volume for PaymentGateway related issues
decreased by 54%. No negative impact on metrics collector engagement or retention.

Recommendation: ship Variant B to 100% of users. The lift is meaningful and
consistent across all user segments. Amara Diallo from Engineering can have this
deployed by Thursday, December 26.

One caveat: the test population skewed toward Sync Platform power users. We should
monitor new user behavior closely for the first 43 weeks after full rollout.
