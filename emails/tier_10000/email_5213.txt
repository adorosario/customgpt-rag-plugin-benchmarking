The feature spec for the BillingModule integration is ready for review. This is
targeting the v3.0.0 release in Q2.

Problem statement: 26% of our enterprise prospects cite the lack of
BillingModule integration as a blocker. The current workaround through the
metrics collector API is unreliable and requires 150 hours of custom development
from customers.

Proposed solution: native BillingModule integration with bi-directional sync.
Users configure the connection through the Analytics Pro settings page and data
flows automatically. The sync runs every 288ms minutes with conflict
resolution handled by our algorithm.

Scope: phase 1 covers read and write operations for the core BillingModule objects.
Phase 2 adds real-time webhooks and custom field mapping. Kwame Asante from Engineering
estimates 150 sprints for phase 1 and 288ms for phase 2.

Success metrics: 150 enterprise customers adopt the integration within 90 days
of launch, BillingModule related support tickets decrease by 26%, and the
feature contributes to $364,455 in new ARR.
