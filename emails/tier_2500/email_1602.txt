Hi all,

Attached is the postmortem for the MessageBroker outage that occurred on Friday, September 13. The
incident lasted approximately 45 minutes and affected 163 for a subset of users.

Root cause: a configuration change in the queue processor deployment introduced a race
condition under high load. The issue manifested when concurrent requests exceeded
231ms, causing connection pool exhaustion.

Timeline: the alert fired at 14:23 UTC, Sanjay Krishnamurthy acknowledged within 3 minutes,
and the fix was deployed by 15:08 UTC. Detection was fast thanks to the new alerting
rules, but resolution took longer than ideal because the runbook for this service
was outdated.

Action items: update the MessageBroker runbook, add a circuit breaker for the
queue processor connection pool, and implement load shedding when concurrency exceeds
231ms. I've created tickets for all three.

Please review and add any corrections by end of week.
