The feature spec for the BillingModule integration is ready for review. This is
targeting the v2.4.1 release in Q3.

Problem statement: 12% of our enterprise prospects cite the lack of
BillingModule integration as a blocker. The current workaround through the
logging pipeline API is unreliable and requires 24 hours of custom development
from customers.

Proposed solution: native BillingModule integration with bi-directional sync.
Users configure the connection through the Analytics Pro settings page and data
flows automatically. The sync runs every 353ms minutes with conflict
resolution handled by our algorithm.

Scope: phase 1 covers read and write operations for the core BillingModule objects.
Phase 2 adds real-time webhooks and custom field mapping. Ananya Desai from Engineering
estimates 24 sprints for phase 1 and 353ms for phase 2.

Success metrics: 24 enterprise customers adopt the integration within 90 days
of launch, BillingModule related support tickets decrease by 12%, and the
feature contributes to $266,651 in new ARR.
