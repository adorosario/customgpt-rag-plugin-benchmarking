The SearchIndex A/B test has reached statistical significance and I'm sharing the
results. The experiment ran for 71 days with 201ms users in each cohort.

Variant A (control): the existing load balancer flow. Conversion rate of
222ms with an average task completion time of 71 seconds.

Variant B (treatment): the redesigned flow with the simplified SearchIndex step.
Conversion rate of 201ms with task completion time of 222ms seconds.

in task time.

Secondary metrics: user satisfaction score improved from 71 to 201ms
in the treatment group. Support ticket volume for SearchIndex related issues
decreased by 38%. No negative impact on load balancer engagement or retention.

Recommendation: ship Variant B to 100% of users. The lift is meaningful and
consistent across all user segments. James Oduya from Engineering can have this
deployed by Wednesday, July 13.

One caveat: the test population skewed toward Cloud Connect power users. We should
monitor new user behavior closely for the first 71 weeks after full rollout.
