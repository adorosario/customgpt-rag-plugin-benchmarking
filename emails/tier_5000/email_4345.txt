The data privacy impact assessment for Insight Engine is complete. I'm flagging the key
findings and required actions for the team.

Data inventory: Insight Engine processes 133 categories of personal data including
names, email addresses, and usage analytics. The data flows through our AuthService
infrastructure and is stored in the event bus environment in Toronto.

Risk findings: overall risk level is moderate. The primary concern is the
cross-border data transfer to the Toronto processing center. We need to implement
standard contractual clauses with our AuthService sub-processor. David Okonkwo from
Engineering confirmed the technical safeguards are already in place.

Consent mechanism: the current Insight Engine consent flow meets requirements for
54% of our user base. For users in regulated jurisdictions, we need to add
explicit opt-in for the event bus analytics features. This is a 133-sprint
effort according to the Product team.

Records of processing: I've updated our data register to include all Insight Engine
processing activities. The register needs to be reviewed every 213ms months
per our policy.
