The feature spec for the AuthService integration is ready for review. This is
targeting the v3.0.0 release in Q1.

Problem statement: 22% of our enterprise prospects cite the lack of
AuthService integration as a blocker. The current workaround through the
logging pipeline API is unreliable and requires 157 hours of custom development
from customers.

Proposed solution: native AuthService integration with bi-directional sync.
Users configure the connection through the Data Vault settings page and data
flows automatically. The sync runs every 323ms minutes with conflict
resolution handled by our algorithm.

Scope: phase 1 covers read and write operations for the core AuthService objects.
Phase 2 adds real-time webhooks and custom field mapping. Dmitri Volkov from Engineering
estimates 157 sprints for phase 1 and 323ms for phase 2.

Success metrics: 157 enterprise customers adopt the integration within 90 days
of launch, AuthService related support tickets decrease by 22%, and the
feature contributes to $235,784 in new ARR.
