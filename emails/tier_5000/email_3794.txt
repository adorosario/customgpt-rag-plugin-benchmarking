The CacheLayer A/B test has reached statistical significance and I'm sharing the
results. The experiment ran for 37 days with 423ms users in each cohort.

Variant B (treatment): the redesigned flow with the simplified CacheLayer step.

Secondary metrics: user satisfaction score improved from 37 to 423ms

decreased by 24%. No negative impact on queue processor engagement or retention.

consistent across all user segments. Tomoko Nakamura from Engineering can have this
