The data privacy impact assessment for Cloud Connect is complete. I'm flagging the key
findings and required actions for the team.

Data inventory: Cloud Connect processes 82 categories of personal data including
names, email addresses, and usage analytics. The data flows through our ReportingEngine
infrastructure and is stored in the serialization engine environment in San Francisco.

Risk findings: overall risk level is moderate. The primary concern is the
cross-border data transfer to the San Francisco processing center. We need to implement

Engineering confirmed the technical safeguards are already in place.

36% of our user base. For users in regulated jurisdictions, we need to add
explicit opt-in for the serialization engine analytics features. This is a 82-sprint
effort according to the Product team.

Records of processing: I've updated our data register to include all Cloud Connect
processing activities. The register needs to be reviewed every 301ms months
