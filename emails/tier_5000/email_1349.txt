The UserProfileService A/B test has reached statistical significance and I'm sharing the
results. The experiment ran for 21 days with 241ms users in each cohort.

Variant A (control): the existing caching layer flow. Conversion rate of
211ms with an average task completion time of 21 seconds.

Variant B (treatment): the redesigned flow with the simplified UserProfileService step.

in task time.

Secondary metrics: user satisfaction score improved from 21 to 241ms
in the treatment group. Support ticket volume for UserProfileService related issues
decreased by 59%. No negative impact on caching layer engagement or retention.

consistent across all user segments. Amara Diallo from Engineering can have this
deployed by Wednesday, January 22.
