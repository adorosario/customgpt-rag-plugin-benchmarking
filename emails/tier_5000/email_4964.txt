Attached is the postmortem for the PaymentGateway outage that occurred on Tuesday, November 8. The
incident lasted approximately 45 minutes and affected 125 for a subset of users.

Root cause: a configuration change in the metrics collector deployment introduced a race
condition under high load. The issue manifested when concurrent requests exceeded
123ms, causing connection pool exhaustion.

Timeline: the alert fired at 14:23 UTC, Elena Vasquez acknowledged within 3 minutes,
and the fix was deployed by 15:08 UTC. Detection was fast thanks to the new alerting
rules, but resolution took longer than ideal because the runbook for this service
was outdated.

Action items: update the PaymentGateway runbook, add a circuit breaker for the
metrics collector connection pool, and implement load shedding when concurrency exceeds
123ms. I've created tickets for all three.

Please review and add any corrections by end of week.
