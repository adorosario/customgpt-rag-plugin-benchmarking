The feature spec for the AuthService integration is ready for review. This is
targeting the v2.6.0 release in Q4.

Problem statement: 38% of our enterprise prospects cite the lack of
AuthService integration as a blocker. The current workaround through the
queue processor API is unreliable and requires 180 hours of custom development
from customers.

Proposed solution: native AuthService integration with bi-directional sync.
Users configure the connection through the Sync Platform settings page and data
flows automatically. The sync runs every 282ms minutes with conflict
resolution handled by our algorithm.

Scope: phase 1 covers read and write operations for the core AuthService objects.
Phase 2 adds real-time webhooks and custom field mapping. Mei Lin from Engineering
estimates 180 sprints for phase 1 and 282ms for phase 2.

Success metrics: 180 enterprise customers adopt the integration within 90 days
of launch, AuthService related support tickets decrease by 38%, and the
feature contributes to $63,558 in new ARR.
