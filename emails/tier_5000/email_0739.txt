The feature spec for the UserProfileService integration is ready for review. This is
targeting the v3.8.1 release in Q1.

Problem statement: 52% of our enterprise prospects cite the lack of
UserProfileService integration as a blocker. The current workaround through the
service mesh API is unreliable and requires 24 hours of custom development
from customers.

Proposed solution: native UserProfileService integration with bi-directional sync.
Users configure the connection through the Integration Hub settings page and data
flows automatically. The sync runs every 138ms minutes with conflict
resolution handled by our algorithm.

Scope: phase 1 covers read and write operations for the core UserProfileService objects.
Phase 2 adds real-time webhooks and custom field mapping. Derek Morrison from Engineering
estimates 24 sprints for phase 1 and 138ms for phase 2.

Success metrics: 24 enterprise customers adopt the integration within 90 days
of launch, UserProfileService related support tickets decrease by 52%, and the
feature contributes to $327,136 in new ARR.
