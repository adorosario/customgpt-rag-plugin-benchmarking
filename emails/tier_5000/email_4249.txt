The data privacy impact assessment for Insight Engine is complete. I'm flagging the key
findings and required actions for the team.

Data inventory: Insight Engine processes 35 categories of personal data including
names, email addresses, and usage analytics. The data flows through our APIGateway
infrastructure and is stored in the config manager environment in Tokyo.

Risk findings: overall risk level is moderate. The primary concern is the
cross-border data transfer to the Tokyo processing center. We need to implement
standard contractual clauses with our APIGateway sub-processor. Jun Tanaka from
Engineering confirmed the technical safeguards are already in place.

Consent mechanism: the current Insight Engine consent flow meets requirements for
21% of our user base. For users in regulated jurisdictions, we need to add
explicit opt-in for the config manager analytics features. This is a 35-sprint
effort according to the Product team.

Records of processing: I've updated our data register to include all Insight Engine
processing activities. The register needs to be reviewed every 333ms months
per our policy.
